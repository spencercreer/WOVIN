Our soldiers and their families afford us the freedoms we enjoy each day at the cost of invaluable sacrifices. That's why we want to aid these families after the passing of their beloved service member.

A service organization geared towards serving our military community, WOVIN fulfills the household demands of our veterans' families. As such, we connect a soldier's surviving spouse with certified contractors or volunteers to address a variety of needs. Whether it's plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, yard work, cleanup, or any number of concerns, WOVIN will take care of that need at no cost.
