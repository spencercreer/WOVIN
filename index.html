WOVIN is a service organization that identifies the needs of widows of veterans. We then match up vetted contractors and volunteers to take care of any plumbing, electrical, painting, yard work, carpentry, repairs or cleanup. If a widow of a veteran is in need, WOVIN will take care of that need at no cost.
